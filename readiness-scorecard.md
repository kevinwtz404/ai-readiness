# AI readiness scorecard (v0)

A practical scorecard for teams assessing readiness to adopt AI in production.

## dimensions

1. strategy clarity
2. data quality and access
3. workflow integration
4. risk and governance
5. capability and enablement
6. measurement and iteration

## scoring model

- 0: absent
- 1: ad hoc
- 2: repeatable
- 3: defined
- 4: managed
- 5: optimising

## usage

- score each dimension 0-5
- identify lowest two dimensions as immediate priorities
- define 30-day interventions with owners and metrics

## output

- readiness baseline
- top constraints
- recommended next interventions
- review date (every 30 days)
